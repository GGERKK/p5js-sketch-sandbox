
to launch headless chrome from terminal
from root dir execute: http-server -c-1 -p 8000
then copy:
/Applications/Google\ Chrome.app/Contents/MacOS/Google\ Chrome --incognito --app=http://localhost:8000

also, you'll have to disable cache (while DevTools is open) in DevTools settings
